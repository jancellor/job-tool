Full job description

Java Technical Lead – Finance | Kings Cross, London (Hybrid)
£95,000 – £105,000 base salary, up to 10% bonus
On-site Monday–Thursday, WFH Fridays
  • Full-Time | Permanent

Are you a hands-on technical leader ready to shape the future of financial technology in a dynamic SaaS environment?

We're looking for a J ava Technical Lead (Finance) to take charge of the architecture, delivery, and evolution of a mission-critical Finance Engineering platform. This is an ideal opportunity for someone who thrives on innovation, security, and developing high-performing teams.

Your Role

As the Technical Lead, you’ll own the technical direction and software delivery of the finance platform:

  • Lead the design and implementation of scalable, secure Java-based applications

  • Write clean, performant, testable code and enforce strong coding standards

  • Drive architectural decisions and collaborate closely with architects and DevOps

  • Own system security, automated testing, and CI/CD best practices

  • Ensure smooth release cycles and resolve production issues effectively

  • Use AI-assisted coding tools to boost productivity and efficiency

  • Mentor engineers, run 1:1s, and lead from the front

  • Collaborate cross-functionally with Product, UX, and QA to deliver results

  • Represent the team in technical forums and influence engineering culture

What You’ll Bring

  • 10+ years’ experience in Java engineering (B2B or B2C SaaS)

  • 4+ years’ experience in technical leadership roles

  • 2+ years working in FinTech or payments systems

  • Proven background with:

    • Java, Spring Boot, Hibernate, Maven

    • REST APIs and microservices

    • Event-driven architecture (Kafka, RabbitMQ, Pulsar, or SQS)

    • PostgreSQL and NoSQL databases

    • AWS and cloud-based environments

    • TDD, BDD, CI/CD pipelines (Git, Jenkins, Docker)

  • Solid grasp of software design patterns, systems thinking, and scalability

  • Exceptional communication skills – able to bridge technical and business discussions

  • A strong mentor with a positive, pragmatic leadership style

What’s on Offer

  • Competitive salary: £95K–£105K (DOE)

  • 25 days’ holiday + bank holidays + Christmas shutdown

  • Private Medical Insurance

  • Life Assurance & Critical Illness Cover

  • Company pension scheme

  • Employee Assistance Programme

  • Regular social events and professional development support

  • Onsite perks: stocked kitchen, fruit, snacks, great coffee

    • Bonus up to 10%

Location & Working Pattern

  • Modern offices in Kings Cross, London

  • Office-based Monday to Thursday; work from home every Friday

❤️ We Welcome All Backgrounds

Even if you don’t meet every bullet point, we’d still love to hear from you. Diverse teams build better software. If you're passionate about delivering secure, impactful financial platforms, we encourage you to apply.

Ready to lead a high-impact finance tech team? Apply now and make your mark!
